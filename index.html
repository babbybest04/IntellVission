Intell vission

Founded by veteran reporters Jan Larson McLaughlin and David Dupont and web developer John Zibbel, BG Independent News is a digital source for free local news stories and arts coverage, as well as campus and community information for the Bowling Green area. Our mission is to provide free news and quality journalism by professional, experienced journalists committed to the community of Bowling Green. The Community Voices and Community Opinion pages allow residents to share community and business updates as well as points of view via letters to the editor.
